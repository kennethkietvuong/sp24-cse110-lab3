How to spread Democracy

There are many ways to spread Democracy. For Helldivers, the main approach of spreading liberty and freedom is through the liberation of planets.
Such planets are filled with vile extraterrestrial lifeforms that want to strip our freedom away.

The Ministry of Truth has identified two extraterrestrial factions that oppose Super Earth: the Terminids and the Automatons.
It is the Helldivers' duty to fight and eliminate these lifeforms for good.
